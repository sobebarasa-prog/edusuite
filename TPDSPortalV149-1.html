Connect your school to Supabase. Teachers automatically use this school connection — they do not need to set up their own. Each teacher's data is isolated: teachers cannot see each other's records.
🔑 You need both keys from your Supabase project → Settings → API:
• Anon/Public key — share with teachers so they can log in on other devices.
• Service Role key — keep private (admin only). Required to view all teachers' records.
